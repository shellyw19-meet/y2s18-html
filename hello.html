A little bit information about the best show ever

FRIENDS is an american TV show which aired on NBC from September 22, 1994 to May 6, 2004, listing ten seasons. The series follow the lives of six close friends: Rachel, Monica, Pheobe, Ross, Chandler and Joey in Manhattan. This show is one of the most popular sitcoms on the NBC network.
